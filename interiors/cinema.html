About

Home Cinema, Ahmedabad

Bootstrap Carousel
Home cinema is an intimate family space, in an existing residence of goldsmith. This space earlier was fragmented therefore a continuous inner envelope is made for a unique experience. This envelope is mapped with a pattern generated by simple algorithm. Variation of same pattern in 2D and 3D are used to dissolve the edges of floor, walls and ceiling. Lights are integrated by choosing specific facets. Voids are created within the facets to place equipment. Cutting and numbering each piece is precisely done on a CNC router. Assembly process demonstrates the shift from digital assembly. This is the most challenging as well as interesting aspect of the project.
